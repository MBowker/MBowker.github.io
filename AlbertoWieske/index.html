Legal Decree Concerning Alberto Wieske

Background

In 2018, the District Court of the Dublin Metropolitan District in Ireland issued a legal decree concerning Alberto Wieske, who at the time was residing in Dublin and had professional ties to Microsoft. The decree ordered payment of €809.50.

Mr. Wieske was formally notified of the decree. As of October 2025, the amount remains outstanding.

Decree

Irish District Court System

The decree was issued by the District Court of the Dublin Metropolitan District in response to a small claim. Further background on the structure and role of the Irish District Court system is available from the Courts Service of Ireland. For further information about the small claims procedure in Ireland, please refer to the Courts Service of Ireland or Citizens Information Board.
